Problem

7-out-of every 10 startups fail, not because their product is bad, but because their customer acquisition fails!

They run marketing campaigns, check analytics, and post content regularly, but often they don't do any analysis, don't understand what the numbers mean or how to improve their strategy.

As a result, their content fails to reach the right audience,

leading to poor customer acquisition, wasted marketing efforts, and lost money and opportunities.

Current solutions are too complex

Many of the users have no idea on what to do next. Existing tools overwhelm non-experts with data they can't act on.

Introducing Vestafy

An AI powered platform that provides businesses with a clear roadmap for social media success

  • Real-Time Data Analysis: Live analysis - means your strategy is always based on what's happening now
  • AI Insights: Recommendations built from real data, no guesswork
  • Growth Tracking: Visualise follower and engagement trends across every post you make
  • For non-expertise: Built for non experts

Key Findings

There are several marketing analytics solutions that already exist, but many users find them to be complicated and challenging to use. Throughout the conversations we had with them, after using those tools they still asked "What should we do next? We still need your help!" - which suggests that the real challenge is not access to data, but getting clear ideas on how to act upon it!

Market

Target Segments

Focusing on early stage businesses and organizations with limited marketing headcount

Why Now?

Many people are starting startups, side projects, and businesses nowadays. Even though AI has made it easier to build products, getting and keeping customers is still one of the hardest parts of it. As competition grows and budgets get tighter, founders - business owners need clear, actionable guidance, not just more data or charts, which creates a strong need for tools that help turn insights into real decisions.
